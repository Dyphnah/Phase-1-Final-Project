About Us

Dr. Stella is a highly skilled veterinary surgeon with over 2 years of experience in the field. She obtained her Doctor of Veterinary Medicine degree from the University of Nairobi, where she was trained in both small and large animal medicine. Dr. Stella is a compassionate and dedicated professional who strives to provide the best possible care for her patients. She has a keen eye for detail and a deep understanding of animal behavior, which allows her to diagnose and treat a wide range of conditions. Throughout her career, Dr. Stella has gained extensive experience in performing surgeries, including spays, neuters, and complex orthopedic procedures. She is committed to staying up-to-date with the latest advancements in veterinary medicine and regularly attends seminars and workshops to enhance her skills. Dr. Stella is known for her excellent communication skills and her ability to explain complex medical procedures to pet owners in a clear and understandable way. Her dedication and passion for animal care have earned her a reputation as a respected and reliable veterinary surgeon within the community.
